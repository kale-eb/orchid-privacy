Common Questions

How do I pair with my partner?

After signing up, you'll see an invite code on the pairing screen. Share this code with your partner, or enter their code to connect.

How does the wallpaper auto-set?

Orchid uses Apple Shortcuts to automatically set wallpapers. During setup, you'll be guided through adding the Orchid Wallpaper shortcut. Once configured, wallpapers sent by your partner will set automatically.

How do I unlink from my partner?

Go to Settings and tap "Unlink Partner." This will disconnect both accounts.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.
